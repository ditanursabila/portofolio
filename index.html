About D.O Kyungsoo


Do Kyungsoo or commonly known as D.O is a Kpop idol with extraordinary talent. He is known as the main vocalist of the group EXO and also a successful actor. Do Kyungsoo debuted as an idol in 2010 with EXO. Then, he entered the acting world through the drama It's Okay, That's Love in 2014.

WORK

D.O released his first album empathy and he also became the main character in the film titled 100 Day My Princess.
